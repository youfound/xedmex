Xedmex AI new feature: Virtual Trading!

We are excited to introduce you to our Virtual Trading feature, designed to help you familiarize yourself with our platform before diving into real trading with your own capital.

What is Virtual Trading?

Virtual Trading allows you to simulate real trading experiences on Xedmex without risking your actual capital. It's a risk-free way to explore and understand how our Xedmex AI Trader works and evaluate the performance of our trading bot, all while using virtual currency.

Getting Started

When you open an account with Xedmex, you automatically receive 100,000 Virtual Dollars (vUSD). Each vUSD is equivalent to one US dollar, so you start with a substantial amount to experiment with.

How to Use Virtual Trading

Navigate to the Virtual Trading Section: Log in to your Xedmex AI account, and you'll find the Virtual Trading button in your dashboard.

Create Virtual Deposits: You can use your vUSD to create virtual deposits. These virtual deposits will mimic real deposits and work with our trading bot in the same way, as real capital deposits.

Experiment and Test: Once you've set up your virtual deposits, you can cbserve, how the bot performs under various market conditions without any own financial risk.

Evaluate Performance: Monitor and analyze the results of virtual trades made on your behalf. You can assess the performance of the bot, understand how it responds to different market trends, and decide about your trading approach.

No Financial Risk: Remember that all trading in the Virtual Trading mode is entirely risk-free. Xedmex Virtual Dollars are completely free to use and let you check automated trading rules.

Ready to Get Started?

Log in to your Xedmex account today and explore the Virtual Trading feature. It's your risk-free gateway to becoming a confident and successful trader with Xedmex AI, before you get started with real capital.

Please note that all vUSD used in Virtual Trading has no value, is purely virtual and cannot be converted into real funds or withdrawn. Virtual Trading is designed for educational and testing purposes only.

Become Real Trader

When you decide to invest real funds, the Virtual Trading mode, including all vUSD and their trading history, will be permanently removed just after you make your own first deposit. This moment will mark the beginning of your real trading journey with Xedmex AI Trader!

Xedmex AI: Mobile Apps Launch Announcement

10.25.2023

At Xedmex we're working hard, and we want to give you a sneak peek into what's coming up - the Mobile Apps!

Launch Date: Q1 2024

Mobile apps for both Google Play and Apple App Store are well underway and are set to launch in the first quarter of 2024.

What you can expect:

1. Impeccable Quality: We're committed to delivering a smooth experience for our users. Our development team is rigorously testing and refining the apps UX to set the highest standards.

2. User-Centric Features: We listen to our community feedback and are incorporating some cutting-edge new features to enhance you trading experience with Xedmex. It's all about making trading even easier and more enjoyable.

3. Cross-Platform Compatibility: Developing for both Android and iOS is a complex task, but it's worth it. We want to make sure everyone can enjoy our apps, no matter what device they use.

4. Security and Privacy: We're taking data protection seriously. Our team is implementing state-of-the-art security measures to keep your funds and personal information safe - always.

5. Instant Access: Our app will be launched as the ultimate, fully-refined product. No demos, trials or promises, but 100% ready to go product on both platforms - Apple Store and Google Play. Thanks to our dedicated dev team and selected users who will be testing the app during development time, you can expect nothing but the best right from the start!

Stay tuned for more updates as we get closer to the launch date!
